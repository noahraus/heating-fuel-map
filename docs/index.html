About this map

Share of occupied housing units using each fuel as their primary heating source, at the census-tract level.

DataACS 5-year estimates, Table B25040
Years2010–2024
GeographyCensus tracts, contiguous US
SourceU.S. Census Bureau
FuelsGas = piped utility gas  |  Elec = all electric  |  FO = fuel oil & kerosene  |  Other = bottled/LP gas, wood, coal, solar, other fuel & no fuel

Gray tracts have no occupied housing units (water bodies, military reservations, parks, airports).
